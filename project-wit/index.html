The Project

About
Project WIT

Attack on Titan: Declaration of War is a fan-produced animated recreation of one of the most pivotal arcs in the Attack on Titan saga — fully voiced, fully animated, and produced without studio backing.

Built entirely on community passion and self-funded production, Project WIT has spent three years assembling a crew of dedicated animators, voice actors, composers, and artists to bring this arc to life with cinematic fidelity.

From storyboards to final composite, every frame is crafted with respect for Hajime Isayama's vision — and an ambition to prove what independent fan animation can achieve when talent and determination converge.

No sponsorships. No monetization. Pure craft.
