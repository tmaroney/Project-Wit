ABOUT
PROJECT WIT

Attack on Titan: Declaration of War is a fully self-funded fan animation project dedicated to bringing the iconic Declaration of War scene from Attack on Titan to life in the beloved Season 3 art style.

Partnered with and co-produced by Studio Eclypse, this passion project represents hundreds of hours of animation, sound design, and production work — created entirely by fans, for fans.

Known across platforms as Project WIT, the series has accumulated over a million views and a loyal following of 7,000+ across all channels, proving that community-driven animation can stand shoulder to shoulder with professional productions.
